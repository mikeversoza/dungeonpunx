📜 How It Works
1
Pick Your Code
Enter any 4-character code — your goblin's name on the battlefield. No one else can take it once you've claimed it.
2
Choose Your Flank
Join the Left, Center, or Right zone. This matters — the boss can target a whole zone at once with area attacks.
3
Wait for the Boss to Strike
The Boss goes first each round. Watch out for where they target — then brace for impact.
4
Choose Your Action — Fast
You have 30 seconds to pick Attack, Defend, or Heal and hit Confirm. If time runs out your last action repeats automatically.
5
Fight Until One Side Falls
Kill the boss before it kills all of you. Every goblin that survives wins. Every goblin that falls... doesn't.
⚔ Your Three Actions
⚔️
Attack
Roll 1d6 damage against the boss. If you defended last round, you get a bonus +3 to your roll — your guard opens perfectly into a strike.
🛡️
Defend
Take half damage from boss attacks this round. Smart when you know an area attack is coming your way.
💚
Heal
Recover 1d4 HP. Can't overheal past your max. Helps keep yourself — and your zone — in the fight.

⚠ Danger — Do Not Clump Up!
The boss's area attacks hit everyone in a zone at once. If your entire horde piles into Center, a single attack can devastate the whole group. Spread out across all three zones to limit how much damage any one attack can do. A split horde is a surviving horde.
💡 Tactics & Tips
🛡→⚔ The Block-and-Strike Combo
Defending last round grants you +3 to your next Attack roll. Alternating Defend → Attack is a powerful rhythm — you limit damage while setting up a harder hit.
💚 Zone Healing Saves Lives
If a goblin in your zone used Heal, any fallen comrades in that zone get advantage on their revival rolls. Keep a healer in each zone if you can.
💀 Death Isn't Final — Yet
When you fall, you get 3 chances to get back up. A successful roll brings you back with a sliver of HP. Fail all three and you're out for good.
⏱ Don't Freeze Up
You have 30 seconds. If you do nothing, your last action repeats automatically. Make sure you set a good default — getting caught attacking when you're about to take a big hit hurts.
